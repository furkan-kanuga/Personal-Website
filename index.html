IoT Based Smart Toll-Plaza

Lead a team of 4 and developed a system which helps the user to get pre-booked toll tickets and thus remove the congestion caused by conventional toll.

For this we made a website for user to book tickets on. The camera at tolls identifies the vehicle with the help of Number Plate Recognition and verify the booking in the database.Upon Conformation a signal is sent to NodeMCU which open the barrier for the vehicle to pass.
